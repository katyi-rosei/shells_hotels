The Area

The name Honolulu means'sheltered harbor' in its native language which has (over time) become sparsely used and now locals speak a language that is a mix of Hawaiian and English. Honolulu city is the capital of Hawaii and is on the island of Oahu. It is the largest city in Hawaii and also a major sea port. It is located 20 miles from Disneyland. During your visit you can visit Pearl Harbour, Hanauma bay and many more fasinating sites.

The Hotel

Our hotel is all inclusive and you can expect to find our outstanding restaurant, which has been awarded 3 Michelin stars. On the site there is also access to a private beach, multiple pools, a family friendly waterpark, a relaxing spa and much more.

The Rooms

Our magnificent rooms include bed and breakfast along with quality room service included in the price of your stay. They also include a balcony that looks out onto the sea. The rooms also come with free wifi and access to a television.
